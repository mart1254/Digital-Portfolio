Video Assignment

Camera Work

Description

For this project, I worked to create a video between 1 minute 30 seconds and 3 minutes in length. I created a video where my subject (me) tries to fall asleep but wakes up to the sound of a faucet dripping water and can't go back to sleep. The video required careful attention to quality, including exposure, white balance, framing, focus, and movement, with the option to incorporate stylistic choices like desaturated elements and color shifts. This creative video featured a voiceover and music, incorporating various post-production techniques such as color correction and balanced audio. I conducted production meetings, and created detailed pitch decks outlining my production design, schedule, story, mood, and team organization. The final video was uploaded to YouTube.
